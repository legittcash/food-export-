Let's Clear the Confusion

It's Not About Big Ships, It's About Someone You Love

The word "exportation" sounds big and complicated, like something only large companies with cargo ships and huge capital can do. But that's not the full picture. Food exportation is simply sending food to people living abroad. Think about your brother, sister, or friend in the UK, US, or Canada, missing the taste of home. Sending them local food is exportation, and it's a lot simpler than it sounds.
